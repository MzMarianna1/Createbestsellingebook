## ðŸŽ¨ Design Specifications

### **Stock Photo:**
- **Opacity:** 50% (so text stays readable)
- **Subject:** Relevant to parenting, education, learning
  - Parent + child studying together
  - Happy family moment
  - Confident child learning
  - Home workspace
  - Books, learning materials
- **Quality:** HD (1080p minimum)
- **Mood:** Warm, hopeful, relatable

### **Text Overlay:**
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif, high-impact (e.g., Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold)
- **Color:** Deep charcoal (#111111) OR white (depending on photo darkness)
- **Size:** Large enough to be readable even at 50% photo opacity
  - Headline: 72-96pt
  - Subtext: 36-48pt
- **Alignment:** Center (for social proof) OR Left (for storytelling)
- **Line Height:** 1.2-1.4 for readability

### **Brand Colors:**
- Accent elements in neon teal (#0d9488)
- White (#FFFFFF) background if needed for readability
- Deep charcoal (#111111) for main text
